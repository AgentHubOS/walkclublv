Our story

Come for the walk.

Walk Club LV started with a simple idea: a couple of friends, a park, and two easy miles every Saturday morning. Then we'd grab breakfast. Turns out that's a great way to make real friends in Vegas.

No pace pressure, no experience needed — just show up, move, and connect. Walk together. Connect. Grow.
